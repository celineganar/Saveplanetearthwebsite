    Fun Fact!

    The United States Environmental Protection Agency claims that for every pound of rubbish you throw away, you’ll create one pound of greenhouse gases. This is because as rubbish decays, it releases carbon dioxide and methane – which is another type of greenhouse gas even more effective at trapping heat in the atmosphere – creating landfill gas, which is a natural byproduct of the decomposition of organic materials in landfills.
